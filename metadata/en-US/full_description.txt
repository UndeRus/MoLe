If you are into [https://plaintextaccounting.org plain text accounting], this may be your mobile companion app.

Requirements:

* An [https://hledger.org/hledger-web.html hledger-web] instance

Features:

* https, authentication (basic)
* Account list with current balance
* Transaction history
* New transaction input
* Multiple currencies (not yet in the new transaction functionality)
* Multiple back-end instances (only one active at a given time)

The development is still ongoing, here's an example list of what to expect in the future:

* Reports
* More filtering of the transaction list
* Pre-filling of new transaction input from SMS (e.g. from your bank) or QR-code.
* Themes
.
